cookies vs local storage:
------------------------
Cookies: Essential for session management and scenarios where data needs to be automatically sent with HTTP requests.
Local Storage: Useful for storing larger amounts of data on the client side, but lacks built-in expiry and automatic request sending.
Learning Both: While you can often use local storage for simple data storage needs, understanding cookies is important for handling more complex web application requirements.
